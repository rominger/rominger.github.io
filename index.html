My Story

  • 2008-2012

    My Humble Beginnings

    From 2008 until 2012 I studied Computer Science at the Hochschule für Technik in Stuttgart, Germany. Next to learning basic programming and software engineering in general, I solved a lot of equations.

  • 2012-2014

    Becoming A Hacker

    From 2012 until 2013 I worked as a software engineer for Thales Deutschland and learnt how to program. Programming itself was not enough. In 2013 I started working for Verivox and learnt how to develop and shape high quality software.

  • Since 2014

    Transition To A Designer

    In 2014 I moved to Umeå, Sweden, to study Human-Computer Interaction and Social Media. I am learning Human-centered design and how to prototype interaction. Furthermore I can also call myself a "Social Media expert" by now.
