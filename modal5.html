Auroracle

For about one year I was living in Ume√•, Sweden. From time to time you have the chance to see the Northern Lights there. There are a few Northern Lights forecast websites, but those are not very mobile friendly. And the existing mobile applications are overblown with information and very unnecessary graphics. I decided to design my own Northern Lights forecast application. I focused on the necessary information and left out all the rest. The result is very simplistic.

While sketching I realized that the "hourly" tab will not work. The Aurora is very hard to predict and often appears within a few minutes. Therefore I replaced the "hourly" forecast tab with a "short term" tab.
