Auroracle

I am currently living in Ume√•, Sweden. From time to time you have the chance to see the Northern Lights here. There are a few Northern Lights forecast websites, but those are not very mobile friendly. And the existing mobile applications are overblown with information and fancy graphics. I decided to design my own Northern Lights forecast application. I focused on the necessary information and left out all the rest. The result is very simplistic. For those who did not notice, the name auroracle is a mix between Aurora Borealias(aka Northern Lights) and the word oracle.

While sketching I realized that the "hourly" tab will not work. The Aurora is very hard to predict and often appears within a few minutes. Therefore I replaced the "hourly" forecast tab with a "short term" tab.
